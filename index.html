About

Future Innovators' School (FIS) is a digital technology track introduced in Pusat Genius @ Pintar UKM from 2021 onwards.

Why Future Innovators School?

Digital technology curriculum

Technopreneurship program

Accelerated employability program

Project-based learning

State of the art Digital Maker Space

Career immersion in tech industry

Industry certification

Industry mentoring

Digital Maker Space

In FIS, students are able to:

Become an expert in programming with Phyton, PHP, HTML, and others

Develop creativity via game development and 2D Animation

Learn Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity and Mobile App Development

Develop collaboration skills during project-based learning

Mentorship by tech industry experts

Access to LMS for self-learning and online assess

Participate in digital making competitions
